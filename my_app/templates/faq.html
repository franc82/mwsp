Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Q: What does MyWeddingSeatingPlanner.com do?

Answer

MyWeddingSeatingPlanner.com is a webapp that makes it easy for you to create a seating plan for your wedding guests. It removes the regular technical hassles of setting up and maintaining a seating plan. It's easy to use and you don't need to know anything about programming or web design. With MyWeddingSeatingPlanner.com, you can manage a seating plan for your wedding using just a web browser.

Q: Is MyWeddingSeatingPlanner.com really Free?

Answer

Yes! There are no catches - our free plan is completely free! It's not a trial and it doesn't expire.

Q: How do I install MyWeddingSeatingPlanner.com

Answer

There’s no installation required! We host MyWeddingSeatingPlanner.com so you don’t have to worry about installing or upgrading any software. You only need a modern web browser such as Firefox, Google Chrome or Safari and you’re ready to go.

Q: What do I need to get started ?

Answer

You will need:

  • Your guest list
  • A modern web browser (i.e. Chrome, Firefox or Safari)
  • And optionally, a credit card, if you want to print the full seating plan of your guests.

Billing Questions

Q: What's the difference between the Free and Paid accounts?

Answer

Free account holders will have a seating plan that only contain half of the tables. A link will be provided to these users which will allow them to get the full seating plan after paying a small fee.

Q: How can I pay?

Answer

Our Free accounts are free forever and no payment is required. For paid accounts, you will need a Credit/Debit card of some form to pay for your Wedding Seating plan. We accept all major brands of Credit and Debit cards including Visa, MasterCard, Maestro and American Express.
